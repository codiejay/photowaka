The vision of PhotoWaka Africa is to build the largest and most engaging community of photographers and visual storytellers that will be bold enough to change the negative stereotypes of Africa and Africans for good. -OUR VISION

To enable, engage, and enrich photographers and visual storytellers in Africa to acquire the knowledge and skills needed to be successful, so that they can make real impact and career with their skills -OUR MISSION
